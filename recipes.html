Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish with a rich meat sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g Ground beef
  • 400g Canned tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, cook onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes, seasoning, and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti and enjoy!

Chocolate Cake

This moist and delicious chocolate cake is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups Flour
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 cup Milk
  • ½ cup Cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased cake pan and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. Let cool and frost with chocolate frosting!
